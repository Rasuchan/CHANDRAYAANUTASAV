ABOUT THE EVENT

CHANDRAYAAN UTASAV is an intra collegiate event in honor of Chandrayaan-3, India's third lunar mission following Chandryaan-1 and Chandrayaan-2, and its groundbreaking success.Celebrating the exemplary success of the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) in landing VIKRAM on the moon's south-pole,which is said to be the ost treacherous surface on the moon, this event primarily focuses on ovel advances that led to the successful landing of the rover on the moon's surface
There are three events conducted in the realm of Chandrayaan's success.
This event will encompass three sub categories and entrants can participate in either one or all the three events that are listed below:

  • SHORT FILM COMPETITION
  • ART EXHIBITION EXPO
  • POSTER MAKING COMPETITION

THEMES FOR THE UTASAV ARE

  • India on Moon
  • New India on Moon
  • Moon is not far away
  • Lunar journey of New India
  • Self-reliant India's Chandrayaan.

The SVCE Science Club in Association with The Student's Council
Cordially invites you all to an Intracollegiate event
CHANDRAYAAN UTASAV

EVENTS

ART EXHIBITION EXPO

An extravagant Art exhibition event, wherein participants will have to come up with attractive art ideas in relevance to the theme specified for the event.Participants are invited to create models, 3D displays and artworks, and have full creative freedom to decide what they would like to showcase.The participants must create their art works using sustainable materials, and present them in the exhibition, conducted in our college,in offline mode

SHORT FILM COMPETITION

This is an online event. Students are encouraged to create a short film that aligns with the event's themes.The maximum duration for the short film is set at five minutes. After production, participants can submit their videos via google forms.The submitted films will undergo assessment, considering their cinematographic quality and alignment with the event's theme.The winners will be officially announced during the valedictory ceremony of the offline event.

POSTER MAKING COMPETITION

This is an online competition in which participants are invited to create and submit digital posters aligning with the event's themes via a google form.The submitted posters will undergo an evaluation process,considering both their design quality and thematic relevance. Winners will be officially announced during the valedictory ceremony of the offline event.
